Guardian Harvest provides the agricultural produce industry with business practices and strategies to promote long term well-being and sustainability of the People, Environment, and Company” Guardian Harvest, Inc. is a full service food safety consultancy. We are located in Kingsburg, CA and currently service clients throughout the Central Valley. We assist our clients in complying with 3rd party audit schemes such as GLOBALG.A.P., and Primus GFS.
